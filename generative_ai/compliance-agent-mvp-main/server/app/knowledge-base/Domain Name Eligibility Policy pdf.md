1. Interpretation and Definitions
1.1. Except where expressly provided otherwise in this Policy , all terms used in this
Policy , and defined in the .aeDA’s Common Definition Poli cy (as amended from time
to time)  will carry the meaning ascribed to them in that Com mon Definition Policy .
1.2. Except where the context otherwise requires, the fo llowing principles will apply in
interpreting the terms and words used in this Polic y:
a) words importing the masculine, feminine or neuter g ender include any of
them, and the singular includes the plural and vice  versa;
b) clause or section headings are for ease of referenc e only and do not affect the
meaning of this Policy;
c) references to notice mean notice in writing;
d) the Schedules and any appendices or annexures form part of this Policy;
e) a reference to a Party includes its executors, administrators, succes sors and
permitted assigns; and
f) words and expressions importing natural persons inc lude partnerships, bodies

1.3. The following words were used in this Policy will h ave the meaning ascribed to them
below;
“2LD”  a Domain  Name that consists of a single Label under the ccTL D. For example:
“domainname.ae”; and
“3LD”  a Domain Name that consists of two Labels under the  ccTLD. For example:
“domainname.co.ae”.
2. Purpose
2.1. The purpose of this Policy is to define the rules t hat govern the granting of 2LD and
3LD Domain Name Licences.
2.2. These Policy rules do not deal with th e Eligibility of Legacy Domains, and are not
retrospective in affect. Legacy Domains will not be  affected by this Policy or the rules
contained in it, and the Registrant will be entitle d to Renew the relevant Domain
Name Licence or update the information s tored in the Registry System in respect of
the relevant Domain Name Licence at its expiry or t ermination PROVIDED THAT the
Registrant meets the Eligibility Requirements to hol d a Domain Name Licence.

 3. Legacy Domain Names
3.1. All Legacy Domains in the Restricte d Zones will be listed with a status of ”Legacy” in
the Registry at the time of the commencement of the  New Registry Operations.
3.2. No changes to the information stored in the Registr y System relating to Legacy
Restricted Domains will be allowed until such time a s the information as to Eligibility
of the Registrant and the correct Registrant Contac t information in respect of that
Domain Name has been updated. The .aeDA will review  these updates as they are
submitted.
3.3. Unrestricted Legacy Domain Names are exempt ed from the Eligibility update
requirement referred to immediately above however t he Registrant Contact
information MUST be updated prior to any Renewal of  the Domain Name.
3.4. Expiry of Legacy Domain Name Licences:
3.4.1.  Domain Name Licences in respect of Legacy Do mains, with greater than
three years until expiry, at the time of the applic ation of this Policy, will have
their expiry date set to three years from the comme ncement of the New
Registry Operations;
3.4.2.  Domain Name Licences that were provided by UAEnic o r Etisalat where the
expiry date for the Domain Name or the term of the Registration is not set
prior to the commencement of the New Registry Opera tions or the provided
expiry date has elapsed, will have their expiry dat e set to one year from the
date of the com mencement of the New Registry Operations; and
3.4.3.  All other Domain Name Licences will be Eligible for  Renewal one hundred
and twenty (120) Calender Days after the commenceme nt of the New
Registry Operations, or the on the expiry of the ex isting Domain Name
Licences, whichever is greater.
3.5. All Renewals of Legacy Domains will be required to meet the Eligibility
Requirements, in which case the relevant Domain Nam e will be the  subject of a
Domain Name Licence, as issued by the .aeDA under t he New Registry Operations .
3.6. Registrants who believe that they have a legitimate  reason to be exempted from
having the expiry date of their Legacy Domain Licen ce(s) altered in accordance with
the principles set out above, or a legitimate reaso n for not having to meet the
Eligibility  Requirements on renewal, should contact the .aeDA.
4. Domain Name Licence
4.1. There are no proprietary rights in the Domain Name System. A Registrant does not
own a Domain Name, but rather the Registrant holds an exclusive licence to use a
Domain Name for a spec ified period and pursuant to the terms and conditio ns that
govern the Domain Name Licence.

6. Application of the policy
6.1. The .aeDA has the responsibility to ensure the inte grity of the .ae ccTLD is
maintained. The .aeDA will monitor and enforce its Policies as required, and reserves
the right to revoke any Domain Name Licence that is found to be in brea ch of its
Policies or the terms and conditions pursuant to wh ich the Domain Name Licence is
granted.
6.2. Accredited Registrars have the responsibility to ap ply the Policies of the .aeDA.
Applications for a Domain Name must be checked for compliance by the Registrar
before being submitted to the .aeDA Registry.
6.3. Registrants are required, in accordance with their Registration Agreement and the
relevant Domain Name Licence, to ensure they meet t he Policy Requirements, when
submitting their Application. The Registrants are a lso required to Warrant that they
satisfy the Eligibility Requirements during the App lication process.

is found in violation of any of the relevant laws o r regulations of the UAE, and in

8. Eligibility criteria – General
8.1. General Registrant Requirements
The granting of a Domain Name Licence does not of i tself give rise to any intellectual
property or other rights in the name the subject of  the Domain Name Licence, or any part
of the Domain Name. Accordingly, it is the Registra nt’s responsibility to ensure that it has
the right to use the Domain Name and that the regis tration or use of the Domain Nam e
does not violate any third party intellectual prope rty rights or other rights, does not
defame any person and does not contravene any appli cable laws.
8.2. Domain Name Licence Period
The Domain Name Licence Period will be as follows:
8.2.1.  one, two, three, four or five years for all Domain Names Registered after th e
commencement of the New Registry Operations, depend ing on the terms of
the particular Domain Name Licence; and
8.2.2.  all existing Domain Name Licences in existence as a t the commencement
date of the New Regist ry Operations will be determined in accordance with
S e c t i o n 3  a bo v e .
8.3. No limitation on Unrestricted Domain Names
There is no limit on the number of Domain Names in the Unrestricted Zone(s) that may be
licenced to a Registrant, however; each Domain Name  App lication must meet the Eligibility
Requirements and the .aeDA Policies.
8.4. Limitations on Restricted Domain Names
Domain Names that may be Registered in the Restricted Zone(s) are only those that are a n
exact match to, an acronym of or an abbreviation of , or closely connected to the name of
the Registrant’s company name or trading name, orga nisation or association name or
trademark.
Each Domain Name Application for a Domain Name with in a R estricted Zone must meet
the Eligibility Requirements and the .aeDA Pol icies.

 8.5. Composition of Domain Names
Domain Names must:
• have minimum of 2 and a maximum of 63 characters;
• begin with a letter or a number and end with a lett er or a number;
• use the English character set and may contain lette rs (i.e., a -z, A-Z),num bers (i.e. 0-9)
and dashes ( -) or a combination of these;
• neither begin with, nor end with a dash;
• not contain a dash in the third and fourth position s (e.g. www.ab - -cd.ae); and
• not include a space (e.g. www.ab   cd.ae).
8.6. Reserved Names List
8.6.1.  The .aeDA maintains the Re served Names List. Domain Names on this List
may not be licenced without the required authorisat ion in accordance with
the .aeDA’s Reserved Names Policy.
8.6.2.  The .aeDA reserves the right to revoke a Domain Nam e Licence, the
Registrant of which is Domain Name i n breach of this Policy or any other
.aeDA Policy.
